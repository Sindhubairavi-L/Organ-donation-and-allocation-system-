Frequently Asked Questions

What is organ donation?

Organ donation is the process of giving an organ to someone who needs a transplant.

How do I become a donor?

You can become a donor by signing up on your country's organ donor registry.

Is there an age limit to donate organs?

No, people of all ages can be organ donors. Your medical condition at the time of death is what matters.
